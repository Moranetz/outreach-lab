About the empirical deserts. The Atlas catalogs 39 named closing techniques. 15 of them — including the assumptive close, alternative-choice close, summary close, trial close, Ben Franklin close, mirroring, accusation audit, multi-threading, and SPIN's deepest two question forms — have zero peer-reviewed empirical studies meeting standard inclusion criteria. They get taught anyway. Outreach generated by outreach-lab does not lean on those techniques by default.
